Account Deletion Request

App: Roamly - Easy Travel

Package Name: com.xiaoyouyou.roamly

Developer: Benevolence_TC

This page explains how users of Roamly - Easy Travel can request deletion of their app account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us by email:

Email: tcheng147852@gmail.com

In your email, please include the following information so that we can identify your account:

  • Your registered email address or username
  • Your request to delete your Roamly - Easy Travel account
  • Any additional information that may help us identify your account

What Data Will Be Deleted

After verifying your request, we will delete your app account and personal data associated with that account, including account profile information and user data stored by the App, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory reasons.

Data Retention

We will process account deletion requests within a reasonable period after receiving and verifying the request. Some limited data may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or other legitimate business purposes.

Delete Data Without Deleting Account

If you want to request deletion of some personal data without deleting your account, you may also contact us at the email address above and describe your request.
